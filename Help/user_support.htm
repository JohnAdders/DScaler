User Support  
 

Currently, most for support DScaler is provided through the AVS Home Theater PC Forum, the largest such forum on the internet and a great place to pick up information and advice. Please feel free to stop by and say hello. You can post questions, bug reports, and suggestions there. We suggest that you do a search of the forum to ensure that your question has not already been answered. Bugs can also be posted at SourceForge, this list should reflect the current known bugs.  Please try to give as much information as possible when describing your problem.

Support at AVS is given by the helpful members of the community, whether they be involved in this project or not. However, some people that may appear often to answer user questions and who are affiliated with project include:
