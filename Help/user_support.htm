User Support  
 

Currently, most support DScaler is provided through the AVS Home Theater PC Forum, the largest such forum on the internet and a great place to pick up information and advice.

Because of the way DScaler is developed some parts of it are much more stable than others and indeed some parts of the code are considered experimental. The parts of the code currently considered experiemental are:

  • Time-shifting
  • Teletext
  • Closed Capions

Please do not report bugs in these areas to the forum.

Additionally there is the ongoing problem of sound support, please read this explaination of Sound Issues before posting issues relating to sound in the Sound Issues Thread

We also suggest that you do a search of the "Home Theater Computer" forum to ensure that your question has not already been answered. Bugs can also be posted in the DScaler bugs page at SourceForge, this list should reflect the current known bugs.  Please try to give as much information as possible when describing your problem.

Support at AVS is given by the helpful members of the community, whether they be involved in this project or not. However, some people that may appear often to answer user questions and who are affiliated with project include:
